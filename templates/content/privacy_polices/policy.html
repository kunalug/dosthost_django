PRIVACY POLICY:

It is essential that you read this Privacy Policy prior to purchasing any Service(s) from Dost Host.

Your privacy is very important to us. This policy explains the information we collect from you, what we do with that information, and our information security practices when you use our web hosting services.

Information Collected

  1. Personal Information: We collect personal information when you register for our services, including your name, email address, phone number, billing address, and payment information. We also collect technical information such as IP addresses, domain names, and server logs.

  2. Automatically Collected Information: We automatically collect certain information about your device and usage patterns, including browser type, operating system, referring URLs, and pages visited on our website for service improvement and security purposes.

Information Use

  1. Service Delivery: We use your personal information to provide, maintain, and improve our web hosting services, process payments, communicate about your account, and provide customer support. This includes server management, domain registration, and technical assistance.
  2. Communication: We use your contact information to send service-related notifications, billing statements, security alerts, and promotional communications (which you can opt-out of). We may also contact you for customer feedback and service improvements.
  3. Legal Compliance: We may use your information to comply with applicable laws, regulations, legal processes, or governmental requests, and to protect our rights, property, or safety and that of our users and the public.

Cookies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience, analyze website traffic, and personalize content. These cookies help us remember your preferences, maintain your login sessions, and improve our website functionality.

You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences. However, disabling certain cookies may limit your ability to use some features of our website. We use both session cookies (deleted when you close your browser) and persistent cookies (stored on your device for a specified period).

Log Files

Our servers automatically collect and store information in log files, including IP addresses, browser types, internet service providers, referring/exit pages, operating systems, date/time stamps, and clickstream data. This information is used for system administration, security monitoring, and service optimization.

Log files help us identify and resolve technical issues, prevent unauthorized access, and analyze usage patterns to improve our hosting services. This data is typically retained for security and operational purposes as outlined in our data retention policy.

Compliance with Applicable Law

Dost Host complies with applicable data protection laws, including but not limited to the Information Technology Act, 2000, and other relevant Indian privacy regulations. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data.

We may disclose your information when required by law, court orders, or government regulations. We will make reasonable efforts to notify you of such requests unless prohibited by law or when we believe disclosure is necessary to prevent harm.

In case of international data transfers, we ensure adequate protection measures are in place to safeguard your personal information in accordance with applicable privacy laws and regulations.

Surveys

We may occasionally conduct customer satisfaction surveys to improve our services. Participation in these surveys is voluntary, and any information collected will be used solely for service improvement purposes and customer experience enhancement.

Survey responses are typically anonymized and aggregated. If personal information is collected through surveys, it will be handled in accordance with this privacy policy and used only for the stated survey purposes.

Your data is stored on secure servers located in India and may be backed up to secure offsite locations.
